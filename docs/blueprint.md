# **App Name**: EcoPulse

## Core Features:

- Climate Data Integration & Storage: Connect to external real-time climate APIs (e.g., NOAA, NASA) to fetch, store, and process diverse environmental datasets for historical and live visualization.
- Interactive Charting Module: Display key climate indicators (e.g., temperature, precipitation, CO2 levels) using dynamic and customizable line, bar, and pie charts with zooming and panning capabilities.
- Advanced Filtering and Time-Series Analysis: Enable users to precisely filter climate data by geographic region, specific date ranges, and relevant parameters for in-depth comparative analysis.
- AI-Powered Insight Generation Tool: A generative AI tool that analyzes selected climate data to identify significant trends, anomalies, and potential future impacts, offering concise summaries for policy or research.
- Report Generation & Export: Allow users to compile and export custom reports based on their chosen visualizations and AI-generated insights, in formats such as PDF or image files.

## Style Guidelines:

- The chosen color scheme is dark, reflecting the serious nature of climate data while enhancing clarity. The primary color, a deep and resolute blue (#1B4598), instills confidence and depth, suitable for scientific and policy-related content. It is derived from the concept of clarity and informed action.
- The background color is a very dark, subtly blue-tinted gray (#16181D). This choice provides a high contrast for the data visualizations and reduces eye strain during prolonged analysis, complementing the dark scheme effectively.
- The accent color is a vibrant cyan-blue (#4CE5CD), strategically chosen to highlight key data points, interactive elements, and call-to-action buttons. Its analogous position on the color wheel ensures harmony with the primary blue while offering excellent visual distinction.
- The primary font for both headlines and body text will be 'Inter' (sans-serif). Its neutral, modern, and highly legible design ensures clear presentation of complex climate data and research findings, suitable for policymakers and researchers alike.
- Utilize clean, modern, line-based icons for navigation, filtering, and data manipulation. Icons should be clear and universally recognizable, avoiding unnecessary ornamentation to maintain a professional aesthetic.
- Adopt a clean, grid-based layout for dashboards, prioritizing generous spacing between data modules. The design will be responsive, ensuring optimal data readability and interactive functionality across various screen sizes for desktop and tablet users.
- Incorporate subtle and smooth transitions for chart rendering, data updates, and filter application. Animations should enhance user understanding and interaction without being distracting, reinforcing a professional and efficient user experience.
